Connect with Yale Alumni Organizations

Looking to expand your Yale alumni network? Connect with these established Yale alumni organizations that serve the Puerto Rican and Latino communities. These groups offer additional opportunities for networking, mentorship, and community engagement.

Yale Club de Puerto Rico

The official Yale alumni club serving Puerto Rico. Connect with fellow Yalies on the island for networking events, community service opportunities, and social gatherings that celebrate our shared Yale experience and Puerto Rican heritage.

Yale Alumni Site @yaleclubpr

Yale Latino Alumni Network (YLAN)

A Shared Interest Group (SIG) that connects Latino alumni across all Yale schools. YLAN provides professional development, mentorship opportunities, and community events that celebrate and support Latino excellence within the Yale alumni community.

Official Website About YLAN Facebook Group
