DB Yale Alumni Network

Our alumni network is a lifelong community of Boricua leaders. Connect with fellow Puerto Rican Yale graduates for mentorship, professional opportunities, and cultural events.

Featured Alumni

Hon. Eduardo Padró

Class of 1975

Co-founder of Despierta Boricua at Yale in 1972 and former New York State Supreme Court Justice. As an undergraduate, he helped establish DB and advocated for Puerto Rican students on campus.

Justice Sonia Sotomayor

Yale Law School 1979

Supreme Court Justice who advocated for Latino students while at Yale Law School, where she served as an editor of the Yale Law Journal and spoke out for the rights of Latino students.
