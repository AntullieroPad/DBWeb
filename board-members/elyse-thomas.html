Elyse Thomas

Event Coordinator

Elyse is a senior in Branford College from Miami, Florida. She proudly identifies as both Boricua and Jamaican ("island gyal down!"), with her Puerto Rican side of the family living just outside of San Juan.

As DB Yale's Event Coordinator, Elyse orchestrates everything from cooking nights to cultural showcases, ensuring every DB event feels like a true familia gathering. Her natural talent for bringing people together creates memorable experiences for the entire Yale community.

Beyond her work with DB Yale, Elyse is actively involved in WORD: Performance Poetry at Yale and Danceworks. In her free time, she takes vocal jazz lessons and reads tarot for friends and fellow students.
