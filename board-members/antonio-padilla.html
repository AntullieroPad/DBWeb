An active student athlete, Antonio can often be found at the PWG (Payne Whitney Gymnasium) working out, practicing with the wrestling team, or training in Taekwondo. He balances his physical pursuits with his role as First Year Mentor for DB Yale.

"I'm a first-year peer liaison at DB, meaning you can come to me for mentoring, guidance, and absolutely any other personal dilemma you need help with," Antonio explains. He takes his mentorship role seriously, connecting incoming Yalies with the DB community while also managing DB's digital presence.

Antonio describes himself as "a hip-hop, rock head exploring a wide range of music," with recent interests spanning from Radiohead and Red-Hot Chili Peppers, to Brockhampton and Clipse, to Marvin Gaye. His personal favorite artists are Kid Cudi and Kendrick Lamar.

His summer reading list reveals his literary interests: "White Night + Notes From Underground, Part Time Indian, Maus, The Alchemist, Heart of Darkness, The Pearl, Tuesdays With Morrie, The Metamorphosis, and Crucibles (currently)."

Perhaps most intriguing is Antonio's collection of unusual hobbies: "I'm a hobbyist who picks up random stuff: self-taught oboe-ocarina-harp playing, book and word collecting, slacklining, and lockpicking (successfully)." He jokingly advises, "Keep your bookshelves locked up around me."

Beyond his official role, Antonio is known to "chef it up for any and everyone who is hungry," combining his culinary skills with his natural hospitality to nurture the DB Yale community.
