Why Collaboration Matters

At DB Yale, we're dedicated to fostering collaborations that spark critical conversations and actionable insights. From educators to community organizers, we welcome every perspective.

Through our network of partners, we've created impactful programs, educational resources, and community events that celebrate Puerto Rican heritage and address important issues.
